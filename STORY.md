# Skitterlings — The Story

## The short version (what's on the title screen)
Every skitterling is a spark of the **Glimmer** — the first light that broke into a hundred worlds. Chase it home: you'll never quite catch it, but the chase wakes the worlds and calls new skitterlings out of hiding.

## The lore (for the studio / future content)

In the beginning there was one light, small and shy and impossibly bright. It was called the **Glimmer**, and it loved nothing more than to be chased. So it ran — and where it ran, it scattered. Each place a spark landed cooled into a **world** (a hundred of them, from the Verdant Meadows to the Celestial Spire), and each spark that touched the ground blinked awake as a **skitterling** — a tiny, round-eyed creature with the Glimmer's same happy itch to *run after the light.*

No skitterling has ever caught the Glimmer. Just when one gets close enough to see its shimmering tail bobbing over the next hill, the Glimmer giggles and **bolts ahead**, and off it goes lighting the next world. The little ones don't mind. They've worked out the secret the Glimmer was always teaching:

> The chase is the point. Running keeps the worlds bright, and every world you light wakes a new skitterling who was hiding in the dark, waiting to be chased into being.

So your skitterling runs. It leaps the world's tumbledown bits, scoops up the warm coins the Glimmer drops like crumbs, and keeps that flickering tail in sight for as long as it can. The farther you go, the more worlds wake, the more friends come out to join the chase — the hundreds of skitterlings you collect are all sparks you helped call home.

You will never catch the Glimmer. That's a promise, and a kindness. Keep chasing.

## How the story shows up in the game (today)
- **The Glimmer** appears in-run as a glowing, fox-tailed creature that teases into view at the right edge — you seem to gain on it (it even glances back), then it speeds up and is gone again. Never catchable, by design.
- **The journey** through 100 worlds = the Glimmer lighting world after world as you survive.
- **Collecting skitterlings** (the gacha/roster) = "waking new skitterlings out of hiding."
- **Coins** = the crumbs of light the Glimmer drops.
- Title screen carries the one-line myth; first time the Glimmer appears, a banner says **"Chase the Glimmer!"**

Use the launch param: ...?embed=1&origin=https://lucidwinds.com&uid=<LW_UID>
Reason: the uid namespaces our LOCAL fallback save *before* first load, so it has
to be present in the URL at boot. The `configure` postMessage path also exists

correctly keyed from the first frame, the URL is the right channel.
Signed-OUT player: omit uid — we fall back to per-browser localStorage, exactly
as you described. Sign-in (uid appears) is what unlocks cross-device.

================================================================
MERGE BEHAVIOUR (our side — so you know what to expect)
================================================================
On `loadState` we MERGE, never blind-overwrite, so nothing is ever lost:
  • owned, themes      -> UNION (a collected skitterling / unlocked world is
                          never removed, even if your blob is older)
  • best*, reached, coins, pieces, shields, magnets, rarityBoosts, dexClaimed,
    dailyStreak, lastDaily, sinceEpic  -> MAX (progress never regresses)
  • coinDoubler, dexComplete, metGlimmer -> sticky true (an unlock stays unlocked)
  • equipped, theme, favorites, muted -> server blob WINS (these are selections)

UNION PUSH-BACK: right after we merge your blob we emit ONE `state` carrying the
merged union. That's intentional — if the player collected skins on this browser
before signing in, that emit hands them to the vault. It will NOT loop: you store
`state` and you do NOT reply with another `loadState`, so it settles in one hop.

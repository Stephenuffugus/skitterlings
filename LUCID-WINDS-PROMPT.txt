TO: Lucid Winds programmer
FROM: Skitterlings (Sky Wolf Studios)
RE: Clean handoff — who owns what, and your remaining checklist

Let's lock the division of responsibility so we stop stepping on each other.

================================================================
THE CONTRACT
================================================================
WE OWN THE GAME. Skitterlings is built and maintained on our side and deploys
itself to ONE canonical live URL:
    https://stephenuffugus.github.io/skitterlings/
Every change we make auto-deploys there. You should treat this URL as a live
service — never copy, snapshot, or self-host the game files. The title screen
prints a build stamp (currently "build 1.5.0-build11"); that is the source of
truth for "what version is live."

YOU OWN TWO THINGS, and only these:
  (A) THE HOSTING SURFACE — where the game is embedded and how big it is.
  (B) ITS PLACE IN THE LUCID WINDS ECONOMY — identity (uid) + sunbeams.

If you do (A) and (B) correctly, you never have to touch the game again — we
ship features, they appear for players automatically.

================================================================
(A) HOSTING SURFACE — your checklist
================================================================
1. POINT EVERY SURFACE AT THE LIVE URL. Both the in-app Sky Wolf section and the
   portal card must load the live URL above in an <iframe>. Delete the frozen
   /satellites/skitterlings/ snapshot entirely. (This single step fixes: stale
   versions, the portal vs in-app version mismatch, and divergent save data.)

2. SIZE THE IFRAME FULL-BLEED + LANDSCAPE. The game fills 100% of whatever box
   you give it. Give it a real one:
       width:100%; height:100dvh; border:0;            (dvh, not vh)
   Skitterlings is a WIDE runner (3.2:1) — the iframe must be at least as wide as
   it is tall, or it gets squished into a strip. Same gapless treatment as your
   internal /play/* games.

3. ALLOW FULLSCREEN. Add to the iframe:
       allow="fullscreen; autoplay"  allowfullscreen
   (If sandboxed: sandbox="allow-scripts allow-same-origin allow-fullscreen".)
   Without this the in-game ⛶ button is silently blocked.

4. ACCEPTANCE TEST: load the live URL directly on a phone in landscape — it fills
   the screen. Match that inside your iframe. Build stamp must read the same on
   every surface.

================================================================
(B) ECONOMY / IDENTITY — your checklist
================================================================
5. PASS THE SAME uid EVERYWHERE. Launch the iframe with:
   https://stephenuffugus.github.io/skitterlings/?embed=1&origin=https://lucidwinds.com&uid=<player_id>&return=<return_url>
   The SAME player must get the SAME ?uid= on both surfaces, or their saves split.

6. SUNBEAMS = PROOF OF ATTENTION. Mint sunbeams host-side from the game's
   heartbeat: { source:"skitterlings", type:"active" } fires ~every 5s while the
   player is actively playing and the tab is visible. Do NOT mint from coins —
   in-game coins are 100% internal to Skitterlings and must never become sunbeams.

7. PREMIUM GRANTS (optional). You can grant premium items the player buys with
   sunbeams/Pi via postMessage to the game:
       grant-doubler · grant-pieces {amount} · grant-booster {kind, amount}

================================================================
ONE DECISION WE NEED FROM YOU — cross-device save
================================================================
Today the collection (unlocked skitterlings) is saved in the browser's
localStorage, so it does NOT follow a player across devices. Steps 1 + 5 make it
consistent on one browser; to make it bulletproof across devices, store the save
on YOUR backend keyed by uid (you already do this for the wallet).
  -> If you want this: say so, and we will add a save-sync seam on the game side
     (emit the save blob on change / accept it on load via postMessage). You then
     just persist that blob per-uid. This is the only way to make unlocks truly
     follow the player everywhere.

================================================================
SUMMARY: everything we've been chasing (stale version, squished/no-fullscreen,
divergent unlocks) collapses into ONE fix on your side:
  iframe the live URL · full-bleed landscape · allow-fullscreen · same uid ·
  (ideally) a server-side save.
Do that and the game runs itself from here. Ping us only for the save-sync seam
or new economy hooks.
